## Overview

Starting in RAPIDS `v26.08`release, scheduled for August 6, 2026 cudf will drop support for Pandas 2 and will upgrade to Pandas 3.0+

## Impact

Users should plan to move to Pandas 3.0+ for RAPIDS v26.08 and later.
